Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 18, 2026. This page explains how GrowShopHigh collects, uses and protects information when merchants use our Shopify app and website.

Legal pages Privacy Policy Terms of Service Refund Policy Data Requests Support

Who We Are

GrowShopHigh provides Shopify content generation, store audit, keyword tracking and AI visibility tools for merchants. For privacy questions, contact legal@growshophigh.com.

Information We Collect

  • Shop information provided by Shopify, including shop domain, store name, plan context and installation status.
  • Catalog and content data needed to provide the app, such as products, collections, pages, blog content and related metadata.
  • User and account information, including names, email addresses, roles, billing status and support activity.
  • Usage, activity and diagnostic logs, including sync events, publishing events, errors and feature usage.
  • Optional connected service data when a merchant connects a service such as Google Search Console.

How We Use Information

  • To connect the Shopify store, sync catalog data and provide requested app features.
  • To generate editable content, audit store content and display visibility or ranking reports.
  • To manage plans, credits, billing status, support, security and troubleshooting.
  • To improve app reliability, prevent abuse and understand product performance.

AI Processing

GrowShopHigh may send relevant store content and user prompts to configured AI providers to generate content or analysis. We only send information needed for the requested feature. Generated output should be reviewed by the merchant before publishing.

Sharing Information

We do not sell merchant data. We may share data with service providers that help operate the app, such as hosting, database, email, analytics, AI processing, billing and support providers. These providers are used only to support app functionality.

Data Retention

We keep information while an account is active and as needed to provide the service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes and maintain security. Operational logs may be pruned periodically. Merchants can request deletion by contacting support.

Shopify Webhooks and Deletion Requests

When required by Shopify, GrowShopHigh supports app uninstall and data request workflows. Merchants may request access, correction or deletion of their data by emailing legal@growshophigh.com or visiting Data Requests.

Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical and organizational safeguards to protect data. No online service can guarantee absolute security, but we work to limit access and keep sensitive credentials protected.

International Transfers

Data may be processed in countries where our infrastructure or service providers operate. By using GrowShopHigh, merchants understand that data may be transferred and processed outside their location.

Children

GrowShopHigh is intended for business users and is not directed to children.

Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy as the app changes. The updated date on this page will show the latest version.
